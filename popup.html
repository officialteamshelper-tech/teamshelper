Teams Helper FAQ

Quick answers for schedules, manual control, privacy, and multiple Teams accounts.

How do Schedule and Manual work?
Schedule changes your status automatically based on your saved times. Manual lets you choose a status yourself until you switch back to Schedule.
Can each account use different settings?
Yes. Each account can use its own status, mode, and schedule.
How precise can schedule blocks be?
Schedule blocks can be moved, resized, and edited to match the times you need.
What does priority do?
Priority decides which schedule block is used when two blocks overlap.
What is No Seen?
No Seen helps prevent other people from seeing that you have read their messages. Toggle it before entering a chat with someone.
Where can I get help or share feedback?
Join the Teams Helper Discord for help, feedback, and updates.
